# SHORTLIST OF 3

**1. Briefkist** — *recommended.* The benchmark survived a full adversarial round untouched: zero App Store/Play/GitHub/PyPI hits, **all four domains free** (.com/.app/.io/.nl), no trademark shadow, no company anywhere using it. "Brief + kist" reads letter-chest in NL, near-parses in DE (Brief + Kiste), and EN speakers can say and spell it on hearing ("BREEF-kist"). The chest motif matches the postmark/"FILED · LOCAL" brand exactly. **Strongest risk:** in Dutch, *kist* unqualified can evoke *doodskist* (coffin) — a mild snicker risk for NL natives; also DE ears may auto-correct it to "Briefkasten" (letterbox), which is at least on-brand confusion.

**2. Brieflade** — the best *story* in the round: a Brieflade was historically the actual chest in which an estate or family kept its deeds and letters (attested in the Deutsches Rechtswörterbuch) — it is literally the pre-digital version of this product, and it is completely unclaimed: 0 App Store results, all four domains free, only antique-furniture and 1860s-archive hits. **Strongest risk:** EN pronunciation wobbles ("lade" invites *laid/blade* readings — fails spellable-on-hearing for EN slightly), and since it's a real (if archaic) German word for a document chest, a DE descriptiveness objection at EUIPO is conceivable — get the manual trademark search done.

**3. Postlade** — clean on every mechanical check with all four domains free; post + drawer is calm and archival, and it's the most neutral of the three across NL/DE/EN. **Strongest risk:** phonetic adjacency to "Der Postladen" (a common German name for postal shops — multiple real businesses) plus the same EN "lade" reading wobble; it's also the least distinctive brand of the three — more category, less character.

**Ranked recommendation: Briefkist → Brieflade → Postlade.** Briefkist earned its spot again — twice-screened, fully free across every namespace including .nl, and the most brand-complete (chest = vault = local archive). If the coffin nuance bothers NL test-listeners, Brieflade is a worthy swap with an even better story. Whatever wins: register .com/.app/.io/.nl immediately (they're all free for both top picks — rare) and run the manual EUIPO/TMview check first. Not shortlisted from the table: Heimpost (*Heim* reads as care-home in DE — "Heimpost" is literally the genre name for nursing-home newsletters, plus .com taken), Postloft (fresh .app registration suggests someone is building under that name), Paperloft (live paper-crafts brand), Letterkist (.nl just taken; weaker than Briefkist at the same idea), Snailfile (clean but utilitarian — reads as a file-transfer tool, not a family archive).
